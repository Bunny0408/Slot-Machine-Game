Rules of the Game:

1. Deposit an amount to start playing.

2. Enter the number of lines you want to bet on (1-3). If you bet on one line only first line will be considered if bet on two then two line and so on

3. Enter the amount you want to bet on each line.

4. Click "Start" to begin the game.

5. If you run out of money, the "Play Again" button will be disabled.

6. Have fun and good luck!
